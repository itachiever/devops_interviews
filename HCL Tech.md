1. Introduce yourself and mention your role and day-to-day activities.
2. Which pipeline and compute are you using?
3. Scenario: You have a Jenkins pipeline and the pipeline is getting blocked due to **security vulnerabilities** detected in scans. The product owner wants to proceed with deployment due to high release pressure. In this scenario, what will you do as a DevSecOps engineer?
4. In the Jenkins pipeline, there is a **performance issue** and the pipeline has become slow (build time increased from 4 minutes to 30 minutes). How will you troubleshoot it?
5. Without making architectural changes, how will you diagnose the issue?
6. Have you integrated **AI/ML** in your pipeline stages for drift detection, latency, or health checks? What AI tools were used?
7. For deployments into **on-prem and cloud**, how are deployments happening using **Terraform or CloudFormation templates**?
8. Share screen and write a simple **Terraform code** to deploy a basic service on AWS. What modules are used?
9. To run this Terraform code, what all **prerequisites** are needed?
10. What are **provisioners** in Terraform?
11. What is the difference between **Terraform provisioners and Ansible**?
12. Can you explain the **Ansible file structure**?
13. When you create a **role in Ansible**, what files are created automatically?
14. In Ansible, there is a folder called **`defaults`** — what is the use of the defaults folder?
15. Can you explain the **basic architecture of Kubernetes**?
16. You are deploying clusters in Kubernetes through Jenkins — can you explain the **integration steps**?
17. Have you done **CI/CD integration and implementation**?
