### My observations from this interview

* This round was **primarily an HR/behavioral discussion**, not a technical interview.
* Only **2-3 technical questions** were asked:

  * CI/CD pipelines
  * Google Cloud experience
  * Kubernetes vs Docker Swarm
* Most questions focused on:

  * Personality
  * Communication
  * Stability
  * Work culture fit
  * Flexibility (office/night shifts)
  * Career goals
  * Teamwork
  * Behavioral scenarios
* The interviewer did not ask any deep DevOps or DevSecOps questions about Jenkins, Terraform, Kubernetes architecture, security tools, scripting, cloud architecture, etc., indicating this was likely an HR/managerial screening after your technical evaluation.
